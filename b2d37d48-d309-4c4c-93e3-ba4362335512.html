Effective Study Tips & Methods

Proven strategies to help you study smarter and achieve better results

Time Management

  • Use the Pomodoro Technique: 25 minutes study, 5 minutes break
  • Prioritize difficult subjects when your mind is fresh
  • Set specific goals for each study session
  • Avoid multitasking - focus on one subject at a time

Study Methods

  • Active recall: Test yourself without looking at notes
  • Spaced repetition: Review material at increasing intervals
  • Feynman Technique: Teach concepts to someone else
  • Create mind maps for complex topics

Exam Preparation

  • Solve past questions under timed conditions
  • Identify and focus on weak areas
  • Get adequate sleep before exam day
